Why DonorPR ?

DonorPR has been the trusted charity, informing, inspiring and connecting charities and donors, with the causes they care about.

A Charity that Supports All in need Charities

We support all charities, no matter how big or small. Offering much more than just donation processing and we enable the fastest disbursements .

 

What We Offer Donors

Free All-In-One Giving Solution

Support any charity and centralize all your giving. A free account allows you to track all your giving, set giving goals, manage monthly gifts, and more.

Many Giving Options

Whether you want to donate once or give monthly, give a charitable gift, donate securities, or fundraise with friends and family, it’s easy at CanadaHelps! Plus, you choose how much information to share with each charity.



Your Dollars Do More

Our commitment to charities ensure your dollars go further. We remove barriers for charities so they can focus on their missions instead of administration. Plus, we offer fast disbursements and weekly direct deposits to charity’s bank account.

We envision a society in which all Canadians are committed to giving and participating in the charitable sector, and in which all charities, regardless of size, have the capacity to increase their impact.
